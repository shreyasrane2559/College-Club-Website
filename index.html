About ACE

ACE is part Of Computer Department of Modern Education Society's Wadia College Of Engineering Pune.
ACE is the club of students which conducts technical and non-technical events that helps to built soft skills and leadership. Through events, ACE provides opportunities for students to enhance their academic, professional, and personal skills. Students can take on leadership roles within ACE, such as organizing events, managing projects, or leading committees, which helps develop their leadership abilities and organizational skills. Being part of ACE allows students to engage in meaningful extracurricular activities beyond their academic curriculum, which can enrich their college experience and help them develop a well-rounded profile. Planning and organizing events within ACE require careful time management, coordination, and attention to detail. Students involved in event management learn how to prioritize tasks, meet deadlines, and allocate resources efficiently, thereby honing their time management and organizational skills. Many events organized by ACE involve teamwork and collaboration,such as hackathons, group projects, or team-based competitions. Working together with peers towards a common goal fosters teamwork skills, including leadership, cooperation, conflict resolution, and the ability to delegate tasks effectively.
